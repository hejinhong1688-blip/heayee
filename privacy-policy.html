HeaYee Smile

Privacy Policy

Effective July 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how HeaYee Smile handles information in connection with our website, international patient enquiries, and authorized social media publishing tools.

Information we may process

  • Contact details and information you voluntarily submit when requesting a consultation.
  • Social account identifiers, page or board identifiers, and authorization tokens provided through official platform OAuth flows.
  • Content selected by an authorized HeaYee Smile administrator for publishing, including captions, images, videos, and scheduled publication times.
  • Technical logs needed to operate and secure the service, such as timestamps, publishing results, and error records.

How we use information

We use information to respond to enquiries, coordinate consultations, operate authorized social publishing, protect account security, troubleshoot failed publishing attempts, and comply with applicable legal obligations. We do not sell personal information.

Social platform access

Our publishing tools use official APIs from Meta and Pinterest. Access is limited to accounts expressly authorized by their administrators. We do not request or store social media passwords. Authorization tokens are stored as protected server-side secrets and are not placed in public website code.

Patient images and health-related information

Patient images, testimonials, or treatment information are published only after the responsible HeaYee Smile administrator confirms that the required permission and review have been completed. Social publishing automation is not used to make clinical decisions.

Sharing and service providers

We disclose information only when necessary to operate requested services, including to authorized dental professionals, infrastructure providers, and social platforms selected by the account administrator. Providers are expected to process information only for the relevant service and security purposes.

Retention and security

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, security, recordkeeping, or legal requirements. We use access controls and protected secrets, but no online system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Your choices and data deletion

You may revoke social platform access through the relevant Meta or Pinterest account settings. To request access, correction, or deletion of information held by HeaYee Smile, contact us through the official WhatsApp link on heayee.com. We may need to verify the request before acting.

International processing

HeaYee Smile serves international patients. Information may therefore be processed in jurisdictions different from your place of residence, subject to appropriate operational and legal safeguards.

Updates

We may update this policy as our services or legal requirements change. The effective date above identifies the current version.

Questions about this policy may be submitted through the official WhatsApp contact link on heayee.com.
