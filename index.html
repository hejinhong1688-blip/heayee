Questions

What people ask before booking

Is this safe? I've heard bad things about overseas dental tourism.

A fair concern. Every material we use comes from internationally recognized manufacturers — VITA, Dentsply Sirona, Ivoclar Vivadent, Wieland — and is QR-code traceable to the source. Dr. Liu Meibo holds internationally recognized DSD and ITI certifications, and your treatment plan and warranty terms are provided in writing before you travel.

What if something goes wrong after I go home?

Your warranty is honored at our Shanghai clinic. If an issue arises within your warranty period, you'll return to Shanghai so the same certified materials and the same doctor who designed your treatment handle the repair — our coordinator will help you plan the trip if this ever comes up.

Do I need to stay in Shanghai the whole time?

No — your two appointments are several days apart, and our team arranges everything in between so you can see the city.

What's included in the price?

Your quoted price includes the initial consultation, X-rays, and temporary veneers during the waiting period. Anything outside the standard plan is discussed and quoted before any extra charge.
